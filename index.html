Welcome

The Positive Psychology Text Analyser allows you to easily add quantitative data to your research by allowing you to quickly compare any text to the World Wellbeing Project's PERMA lexica in English 🇬🇧 / 🇺🇸 or en Espa√±ol 🇪🇸, as well as many of their other lexica. Simply copy any text (transcripts, essays, etc.) into the textbox below, choose any additional options, and press Start!

(Your text is kept private. Analysis takes place in your browser and data is never transferred to, or saved on our server!)

It may seem that the number of matches exceeds your document's word count. This is because a word can be a match in multiple categories. Also the analysis takes into account tokens not words. A token might be a single character or piece of punctuation, whereas the word count is a count of full words.
